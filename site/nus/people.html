Founding Director & LV-Lab

Director
Dr. Shuicheng Yan
Distinguished Professor
(Practice), SoC, NUS

Prof. Shuicheng Yan is a Fellow of the Singapore Academy of Engineering, AAAI, ACM, IEEE, and IAPR. His research primarily focuses on computer vision, machine learning, and multimedia analysis. He has been recognised as one of the World's Highly Cited Researchers for ten years, with an H-index exceeding 150. Prof. Yan began his academic career at NUS on December 21, 2007, later transitioning to industry—serving notably as Group Chief Scientist at Sea Group, among other prominent roles—and subsequently returned to NUS on January 23, 2025.

Under Prof. Yan’s leadership, his team has achieved ten first-place or honorable-mention distinctions in the flagship competitions Pascal VOC and ImageNet (ILSVRC). Moreover, the team has earned more than ten Best Paper and Best Student Paper awards, including a remarkable "grand slam" at ACM Multimedia, a premier multimedia research conference, where they secured three Best Paper Awards, two Best Student Paper Awards, and one Best Demo Award.
